How it works

NoteKeep is designed to be simple and easy to use. Here's how it works:

  1. Create an account or log in to your existing account
  2. Create a new note or list, or import an existing one
  3. Organize your notes by category and color
  4. Set reminders and due dates for important notes
  5. Collaborate with others on shared notes and lists
  6. Access your notes from anywhere, on any device
